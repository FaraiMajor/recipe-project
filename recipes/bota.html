Nhopi(Traditional Porridge) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • ½ Butternut squash Peeled, deseeded and cut into cubes
  • ½ teaspoon Coriander
  • ½ teaspoon Cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon Ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Peanut butter
  • ½ cup Cornmeal

Description

Nhopi is a traditional breakfast meal similar to porridge made with either a pumkin or butternut squash. Nhopi is not only delicious and filling, it is also nutritious. Butternut squash is a good source of Vitamin B6, Vitamin E, niacin, thiamine manganese, potassium etc. The peanut is a good source of protein and other essential nutrients. What's there not to like?

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan under medium heat.
  2. Add the coriander, cinnamon and ginger, and stir continuously for 30 secs.
  3. Pour the butternut squash pieces into the pan with 1 ½ cup of water.
  4. Cover the pan and allow to cook until the pieces are soft.
  5. Stir in the peanut butter and leave for about 3 mins to cook.
  6. Add ½ cup of water into the cornmeal and mix thoroughly to make sure there are no lumps.
  7. Pour the cornmeal mix into the pan and stir thoroughly with a wooden spoon, mashing the butternut squash as you go.
  8. Cover the pan and leave for about 5 mins, until the content of the pot thickens
  9. Leave to simmer for another 5 min then serve
