Sadza and Okra recipe

Description

This special Zimbabwean delicacy, the Okra, also known Ladies’ Fingers, Derere [Zimbabwe] and Delele [Zambia]. An ecellent source of vitamin C, with zero fat, this Okra soup recipe will have your taste buds dancing. Goes together well when coupled with Sadza. Im getting Hungry already...

Ingredients

  • 1½ – 2 cups chopped okra
  • 1 frsh tomato
  • 2 birds eye chilli
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon bicarbonate of soda

Instructions

  1. Pour water and bring to boil
  2. Once boiled, add bicarbonated of soda and stir in to mix
  3. Add chopped okra and birds eye chilli
  4. Leave to boil for about 10 minutes whilst stirring
  5. Add fresh tomatoes, salt and stir in. Leave to cook for another 5 minutes
  6. Leave to simmer for another 5 mintes then serve
